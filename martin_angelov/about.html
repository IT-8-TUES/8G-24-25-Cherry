About

Hi, we're team Cherry. The idea of our site is to give some basic knowledge about programming for the ones that are interested.

Our story

We are students from ELSYS and we are in 8th grade. We are studying programming and we are interested in web development. We got a project in one of our special subjects and decided that with it we are also going to help others learn about programming languages

Our goal

Our goal is to help people learn about programming languages and how to use them. We want to make programming more accessible and understandable for everyone.

Our future

We are planning to expand our site and add more content about programming languages, tutorials, and resources for learning. We want to create a community where people can share their knowledge and help each other.
